program

Duration 3 hours

40% theory, 60% practice. We will tell you about all the features of liquid acrylic, show you the technique, Help you choose and mix colors. as a result you will get a unique picture

Group of 5-6 people

A small group allows the teacher to devote time to each participant of the master class. You will also have the opportunity to meet creative people and make new friend

All materials are included in the price:

Round canvas with a diameter of 40 cm, acrylic paints, protective apron, cups, gloves, varnish to cover the painting and good mood
